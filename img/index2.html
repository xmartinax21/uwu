THE SORTING CEREMONY

The Sorting Ceremony is held in the first school day. Is a ceremony where the students are selected in one of the Hogwarts Houses. THE SORTING HAT The Sorting Hat is an old hat that has been in the castle since Hogwarts was founded. In the first day, the student puts on the hat and it starts to speak and think about which house would be the indicate for him.
